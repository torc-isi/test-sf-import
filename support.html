Support
 
Support for Torc is available through project forums and project trackers.  The Feature Request tracker provides a fairly accurate glimpse of the current Torc development status.

  • Help Forum:  Discuss problems with Torc, and request assistance from the developers or the community.
  • Open Discussion Forum:  Discuss ideas for, use of, or experience with Torc.
  • Bug Reporting:  Report Torc bugs, including specific details and symptoms whenever possible.
  • Feature Request Reporting:  Request new Torc features, or share your wish list.
Note that active participation in these forums or trackers generally requires a SourceForge account or an OpenID account.

Those who are not proficient in C++ may wish to visit cplusplus.com.
